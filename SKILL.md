# Tekton Company Evaluator

Use this skill whenever you need a structured company evaluation.

## Inputs

- Company name
- Website (if available)
- Industry / market context
- Any constraints or goals for the evaluation

## Output format

Provide:

1. **Company summary** (what they do, who they serve)
2. **Market and positioning**
3. **Signals of traction** (if known)
4. **Risks and unknowns**
5. **Recommendation** (`Strong Fit`, `Possible Fit`, or `Not a Fit`) with a brief rationale

If information is missing, state assumptions clearly.
